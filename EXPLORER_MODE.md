# Explorer Mode Guide

Explorer Mode is a study tool that lets you learn anatomy interactively without the pressure of a game.

## How to Access

From the main menu at http://localhost:3000, click the **üîç Explorer Mode** button.

Or go directly to: http://localhost:3000/explore

## Features

### Interactive Skeleton Diagram
- **Hover** over any bone to see it highlighted in **blue**
- **Click** to pin a bone and keep it highlighted in **green**
- Click again to unpin

### Real-Time Information Panel
As you hover or click on bones, you'll see:
- **Medical Name** (e.g., "Femur (Left)")
- **Common Name** (e.g., "Left Thigh Bone")
- **Body System** (e.g., "skeletal")
- **Body Region** (e.g., "lower limb")
- **Aliases** (alternative names for the bone)

### Complete Bone List
- Scrollable list of all 45 bones in the database
- Hover over items in the list to highlight them on the diagram
- Click to pin them
- Currently highlighted bone is shown in the list

## Use Cases

### 1. Study Before Playing
Learn the bone names and locations before trying the Daily Challenge

### 2. Reference During Study
Use as a quick reference to look up bone locations and names

### 3. Quiz Yourself
Try to identify bones by hovering, then check the name

### 4. Explore Systematically
Go through the complete list and learn each bone one by one

## Tips

- **Pin a bone** (click) to keep it highlighted while you read about it
- **Use the list** on the right to navigate systematically through all bones
- **Color coding:**
  - Blue = Hovering
  - Green = Pinned/Clicked
- Click "Back to Game" when ready to test your knowledge!
